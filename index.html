origin

FimmPro was established to be your trusted partner in solving your labour market, employment related problems.


My personal story fueled this curiosity to understand how things work in Finland. How does hiring and job searching happen? Why do we need resources for attraction marketing campaigns and relocation services, when locally we observe high rates of unemployed, underemployed people looking for their spot in the labour market?


I took it upon myself to dig up the truth.


Now I can state for certain: I know Finland's labour market systems, processes, problems well. And I can help you circumnavigate what stands in your way.

mission

FimmPro is on a mission to help people make smarter decisions.


Whether you are hiring, looking for a job, or trying to assist either of those parties as a private or public service provider.

vision

Hiring, job search and related services are purposefully designed and executed by making informed decisions based on proven successful strategies and data.


With that in mind, there is no one-size-fits-all solution.


We follow the example of good practices and tailor them to your particular circumstances.

method

Hiring, employment are serious topics. Life-changing even.

We handle them with a human-centric approach, critical thinking, self-reflection, and a pinch of humour.

About Me

In my heart I am an archaeologist, a detective, or an investigative journalist.

These titles are some of the most precise to describe my drive and way of life. Because I feed on questions.

I combine my passion for facts and artifacts with storytelling, to deliver a well documented interpretation of Finland's labour market for you to use to your advantage.

As a lifelong explorer, I am in perpetual search for explanations to factual and behavioural phenomena.

As a systems thinker, I look for links connecting us into contemporary society which in its totality can only be described as a wicked problem.*

I had my toes dipped into higher education, public and private sectors. Therefore I understand the variety of perspectives and goals sought by different stakeholders.

This broad perspective allows me to design services aiming for efficiency, yet with a human-centric approach. Striving to intensify results of any particular action or process.

This energy is directed at employers, recruitment agencies, job seekers, public service providers. Individual actors connected into a singular net - the labour market.

*Wicked problem as defined by Rittel and Webber in "Dilemmas in a General Theory of Planning".
